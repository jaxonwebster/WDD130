Overview

Purpose

Webster Water Rapids was designed by Mother Nature herself to take you out of your comfort zone and into rapids of Eastern Idaho. We are determined to provide safe, affordable, and memorable fun here at Webster Water Rapids. Rest assured, your safety is our first priority. Adrenaline is our second.

Audience

On our custom state-of-the-art rafts, we typically find adventure-seeking individuals that like to live life on the edge. Ages range from as low as 8, to as high as 70. Whether you're navigating the river with family, friends, or riding solo, Webster Water Rapids has incomparable deals that makes rafting affordable and worth every penny!
