Rivers at Webster Water Rapids

Nestled deep in the Webster Water Rapids Wilderness of No Return winds many wild rivers, including the Salmon river. The Salmon winds through the Utah mountain for 7 miles, inviting adventure at every turn. Herds of Elk and Deer graze on the grass along the river as the sun sets and rises each day. Let's not forget the rapids! They range from class I-IV. As a free flowing river the water is higher, faster, and colder in the early summer and lower, slower, and warmer in the late summer. There are good rapids all summer...but families with children who like to swim tend to appreciate the late summer's slower, warmer water more.
