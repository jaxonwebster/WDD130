About Peachy Penguin

Welcome to Peachy Penguin! We are a family owned business dedicated to making your stickers dreams come true! We offer a variety of stickers guarenteed to satisfy the needs all ages and personalities, or businesses! From motivational quotes to help you get through your day, to quirky jokes to add a smile to your face, we have it all. That empty spot on your hydroflask or laptop is begging for you to dive deep into our one of a kind and hand made graphic stickers! We also offer services to design logos or shipping labels for businesses! Research shows that stickers are one of the most effective ways to increase visibility for branding and promotions. The importance of a good design cannot be understated. We love to work with our clients until they are satisfied with the results and are proud to stick their custom graphic all over town to increase awareness! All of our stickers, no matter the theme, are printed on high quality matte adhesive paper that you will love.
